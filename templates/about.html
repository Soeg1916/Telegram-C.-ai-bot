What is Character Chat Bot?

Character Chat Bot is an AI-powered Telegram bot that allows users to have immersive conversations with fictional characters from various universes. Our bot transforms AI interactions into engaging, role-playing experiences with advanced emotional expression and character consistency.

Whether you want to solve mysteries with Sherlock Holmes, discuss ninja techniques with Naruto, or have a heart-to-heart with your favorite character, our bot provides a unique and personalized experience.

Key Features

Authentic Character Interaction

Each character maintains their unique personality, speech patterns, and knowledge base. They respond as if they are real people with physical forms and complete personalities.

Emotional Expression

Characters can express genuine emotions through specially formatted text. Actions appear in strikethrough text, while emotional sounds are emphasized in bold.

Custom Character Creation

Create your own characters with unique personalities, traits, and backstories. Share your creations with the community through our approval system.

Relationship Development

Characters remember your conversations and develop their relationship with you over time. Their mood and feelings toward you change based on your interactions.

NSFW Mode (Adults Only)

For adult users, characters can engage in more mature and uninhibited conversations. This feature is optional and can be toggled for each character.

Technology

Character Chat Bot is powered by advanced AI language models from Mistral AI, combined with custom natural language processing techniques to ensure authentic character responses.

The bot is built with Python using the Telegram Bot API and Flask for web components. All character data and conversation histories are securely stored in a PostgreSQL database.
