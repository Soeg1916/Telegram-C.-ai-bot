About Character Chat Bot

An AI-powered chatbot that lets you talk to your favorite fictional characters.

Our Mission

Character Chat Bot was created with a simple mission: to bring fictional characters to life through conversation. We believe that stories and characters have the power to inspire, entertain, and connect people across cultures and generations.

By combining advanced AI technology with carefully crafted character personalities, we've created an experience that allows you to have meaningful conversations with characters from your favorite stories.

How It Works

Our bot uses Mistral AI, a powerful language model, to generate character responses that are consistent with their established personalities, backgrounds, and speaking styles.

Each character has a unique set of personality traits that influence how they respond to your messages. As you chat with a character, they'll remember your previous conversations and their mood will change based on your interactions.

Features

  • Chat with preset characters from popular fiction
  • Create your own custom characters
  • Characters remember your conversations
  • Dynamic mood system that evolves based on interactions
  • Easy access through Telegram

Technology

Character Chat Bot is built using:

  • Python Telegram Bot library
  • Mistral AI for character response generation
  • Flask for the web interface
  • PostgreSQL for data persistence

Privacy & Data

We care about your privacy. Here's what you should know about how we handle your data:

  • We store your Telegram ID and conversation history to provide a seamless experience.
  • Your conversations are private and not shared with other users.
  • We don't use your conversations for any purpose other than improving your experience with the bot.
  • You can reset your conversation history at any time using the /reset command.
